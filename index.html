About Leo

The bridge between innovative brands and everyday consumers.

I've always been fascinated by how the right products elevate our daily routines. Whether it's an app that eliminates manual admin, a workspace tool that boosts focus, or a lifestyle brand that improves a morning ritual, I look for things that make life smoother.

With a background in digital product design, I have a sharp eye for what makes a product stand out — and I know how to explain it in plain English. I take confusing features, sleek designs, or unique selling points and turn them into relatable, 30-second stories.

I don't just show a product; I show why it belongs in someone's day.
